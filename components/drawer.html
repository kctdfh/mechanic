Drawer

Pass your markdown here and the prose utility class will render it correctly! the prose class applies all the sizing and padding information to its child elements based on their tag so you don't have to worry about different text appearing differently depending on the elements you have on your markdown. Useful for when you're calling a database for your wiki articles. Just remember to use a server side function to render the markdown to HTML before injecting it into this article tag
